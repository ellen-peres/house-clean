Since 2020, we have been delivering a good cleaning service in our beloved region! We offer people more than just a cleaning service, we are offering sincere relief to those who need our help most, especially on the busiest days when you don't even have time to breathe. Our objective has always been this, and always will be, as we believe that every good service, whatever it may be, must be accompanied by a noble objective that can bring help and relief to those who seek it. We believe that our work can alleviate at least one burden in a busy world like the one we live in.

From the dream of being able to have your own business and provide a decent, good quality service, Lotus Clean was born. We have been serving this region for some time, but now, in order for this dream to become a reality, we have decided to condense our experience and passion into what we call Lotus Clean. Delivering quality cleaning and care for those who have their comfort and tranquility as a priority! And always remember that, by taking care of your home, we are also taking care of you too!
